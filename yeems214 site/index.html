Duffin's Music Player

Technologies: Java, Maven, SQLite, Python, FFmpeg

Works on: Windows, macOS, Linux

Date of Creation: March 2024

This is a little project of mine during my COMPROG12 class under my BSIT course in University of Cebu. This was a simple 10 day assignment where we are tasked to create a WAV Music Player with Time Synced Lyrics functionality only. This has a built in library system powered by SQLite (Beta) with the goal of easing the Music Playback experience and is designed to solve the issue where .WAV songs lack any metadata. It's lyrics formatting utilizes the .lrc format but in .txt form which I found to be the best way to handle song lyrics with ease.
